# SendFlow — Making Crypto Remittances Human

Every month, Ahmed sends $200 home to his family in Pakistan from Dubai. Western Union takes $12 and three days. His family gets less, later.

With SendFlow, Ahmed opens Telegram, types **“Send $200 to my mom,”** and the transfer settles on **Solana** in seconds for cents in fees — with a clear **comparison vs Western Union** so he sees what he kept in his pocket.

Maria in Nigeria invoices a US client who refuses PayPal. She drops a **payment link**; the client pays **USDC** from Telegram without installing a wallet app.

A tutor in Manila turns on **POS** mode, shows a **QR**, and takes **USDC** without card fees.

SendFlow is a **Solana-native AI remittance agent** on **ElizaOS**, running inference on **Nosana** GPUs. It targets three pains wallets ignore: **expensive remittance**, **blocked freelancer payments**, and **merchant card fees**.

The agent creates an encrypted **custodial wallet** per user, adds **MoonPay / Transak** on-ramps and off-ramps, compares fees to **WU / PayPal / wires**, supports **phone-invite receipts** (Twilio), **savings goals**, and the full feature set (stakes, DAO, streams, `/pay/:pageId`, 30s undo, and more).

**47** product capabilities. **28** automated tests. **One** mission: make sending money as easy as sending a text.
